About Our Company

Dalian Hong Xiang International Trade CO., LTD

We are a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality swimwear, established in Dalian, China. Our company specializes in producing premium swimwear for global markets, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology.

With years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for excellence in quality, innovation, and customer service. Our products are exported worldwide, serving customers across different continents.

We are committed to sustainable manufacturing practices and ensuring that every piece of swimwear meets the highest international standards.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional swimwear products that combine comfort, style, and durability while maintaining competitive pricing for our global customers.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted partner in swimwear manufacturing, known for innovation, quality, and reliability.
